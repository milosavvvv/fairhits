Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about membership and our process.

How are picks generated?
▸

We blend proprietary projections with analyst review. Only plays that clear a value threshold and fit bankroll rules make the card.

Do you track closing line value (CLV)?
▸

Yes. We note open vs. closing lines so members can evaluate execution quality over time.

What bankroll strategy do you use?
▸

Unit-based staking with caps per day and per event. The goal is to avoid drawdown while letting edges compound.

Can I cancel anytime?
▸

Absolutely. Premium is month-to-month—no lock-ins.
